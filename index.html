Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Salesforce or HubSpot?

Salesforce and HubSpot are built for B2B sales and lead management. UppalCRM is purpose-built for B2C subscription businesses, with native support for family accounts, device tracking, and consumer lifecycle management.

Can I import existing consumer data?

Yes! Import from CSV, connect via API, or migrate from your existing system. We support all major formats and provide free migration assistance during onboarding.

Do you integrate with my billing system?

Absolutely. We integrate with Stripe, Chargebee, Recurly, and custom billing platforms via REST API and webhooks. We handle relationships while your billing system handles payments.

Is there a limit on consumer records?

No limits. Track unlimited consumer accounts, household members, and devices. Pricing is based on your team size (how many of YOUR employees need access), not your subscriber count.

What happens after the trial?

After your 30-day trial, simply add a payment method to continue with full access. Track unlimited consumers from day one. No automatic charges or credit card required during trial.

Do you offer onboarding support?

Yes! Every new customer gets free onboarding support, including data migration assistance, team training, and custom workflow setup. We're here to ensure you succeed.
